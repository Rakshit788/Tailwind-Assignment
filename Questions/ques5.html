One of the last time I was him, after I had finished writing most of my book, I asked him again about his tendency to be rough on people."Look at results," he replied. "these are all smart I work with, and any of them can get a top job at another place if they were truly feeling brutalized. But they don't". Then he paused for few moments nad said, almost wistfully,"And we got amazing things done."Indeed he and Apple had a string of hits over a past dozen years that was greater than that of any other inovative company in modern times: iMac, iPod, iPod nano, Itunes stores, Apple stores, Mackbook, iPhone, Ipad, App Store, OS X Lion-not to mention every pixar film. And as he battled his final illness, Jobs was surrounded by an intensely loyal cadre of collegaues who had been inspired by him for years and very loving wife, sister, and four children
